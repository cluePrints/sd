This project is about connecting people.

There are 3 prerequisites for usage:
* You have some spare time
* You have something to share
* You are willing to share

Examples: 
1) you have 1 hour next Thursday and you're plumber zealot willing to pass your knowledge to a newbie

2) you'd like to fix some of your kitchen sink plumbing and you're looking for a someone to guide you through

3) it's allowed to share about anything, including JavaScript, Ruby etc

Special thanks to apofig for the inspiration.
